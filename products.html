Origin & Sourcing

From the World's Finest Forests

All agarwood is sustainably sourced under CITES regulations and third-party audited supply chains.

Vietnam
Primary Source · Aquilaria crassna
Vietnam's Khanh Hoa province produces some of the world's most prized oud. Our Vietnamese agarwood is sustainably cultivated under CITES-compliant frameworks, known for its deep, complex, leathery fragrance profile.
Indonesia
Secondary Source · Aquilaria malaccensis
Sumatra and Kalimantan forests yield Aquilaria malaccensis — one of the most medicinally documented species. Rich in chromone compounds with a sweeter, warmer scent and high sesquiterpene content ideal for health applications.
Cambodia
Select Reserve · Aquilaria sinensis
Cambodian agarwood — rare "oud cambodia" — features an exceptional, smooth, fruity-floral oud profile. Reserved for our highest-grade products and collector-tier raw materials.
