Our Vision

A Global Wellness Brand Built on Authenticity

We are building the world's most trusted agarwood brand — one that bridges millennia of Eastern healing wisdom with the rigour of modern pharmaceutical science.

Founded in Seoul in 2020, Agarwooding has grown from a research project into a recognised premium brand with 50+ products, 10+ global partners, and a 98% customer satisfaction rate — all without compromising on quality or transparency.

Our Mission

Four Commitments That Define Everything We Do

01
Premium Sourcing
Stable sourcing of premium agarwood raw materials with rigorous quality management at every step — from forest floor to certified laboratory testing.
02
Science-Based Innovation
Developing innovative products grounded in peer-reviewed science. Our patented extraction method delivers measurably superior bioactive compound preservation.
03
Education & Information
Providing accurate, evidence-based information about agarwood culture, health benefits, and the rampant counterfeiting issues plaguing the industry.
04
Sustainable Production
Building sustainable production systems that protect both the environment and the wild Aquilaria populations that make genuine agarwood possible.

Core Values

What We Stand For

🌿
Nature
Deep respect for the natural processes that create agarwood. We source only from sustainably managed forests and certified cultivation programs across Vietnam, Indonesia, and Cambodia.
✦
Quality
ISO 22000 certification, in-house and third-party laboratory testing, patented extraction technology, and GMP manufacturing standards — quality is non-negotiable at Agarwooding.
🤝
Trust
Full product traceability. Every batch carries transparent sourcing information, species verification, and a complete production history — so customers know exactly what they're receiving.

Leadership Team

The People Behind Agarwooding

Chief Executive Officer

Kim Chul-soo

20+ years of dedicated agarwood research. Kim founded Agarwooding on the conviction that the world deserves access to authentic, scientifically validated agarwood — not the counterfeits flooding the market. He personally oversees every sourcing decision and quality standard.

Head of Product Development

Lee Young-hee

Specialist in ethnopharmacology and natural product chemistry. Young-hee bridges traditional Eastern medicine with modern formulation science — the mind behind our patented extraction method and the developer of our capsule and ampoule product lines.

Customer Experience Director

Park Ji-min

Ji-min's singular focus is ensuring that every customer interaction reflects the care and integrity that goes into our products. From first contact to repeat purchase, she has built the systems and culture behind our 98% satisfaction rate.

Company History
